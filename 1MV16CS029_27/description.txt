Description

To book tickets and learn about the upcoming movies.

A ticket booking site that allows the user to check the seats available and book seats at same time.
Users can make prior bookings of their desired seats according to suitable time and price comfortable by the user.
Users can also cancel their bookings or book a tickets at a differnt time slot.
A system that is user friendly and customize their bookings according to their needs.
